The return of the programme exploring Deaf lives and culture.

Now an annual staple, LSFF presents new short films exploring Deaf lives, culture and language. Submitted from all over the world, these films span genre and form, from experimental animation to thriller, whilst capturing the uniqueness and the universality of the Deaf experience.

**After Dawn**  

Director Yohann Kouam, France 2023, 24 mins

**The Singer**  

Director Cora Bissett, UK 2023, 20 mins

**Nightshift**  

Director Bim Ajadi, UK 2022, 11 mins

**Firefly**  

Director Anne-Marie Scragg, UK 2023, 16 mins

**Senses**  

Director Alyscia Cunningham, USA 2023, 13 mins

This event will have a BSL introduction.

Total running time 84 mins
